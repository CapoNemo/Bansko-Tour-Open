Top 10 attractions in Bansko and near Bansko

  1. Peak Vihren

    Vihren is the highest peak in Pirin mountain with the amazin high of 2915 metters.

  2. Pirin Street

    All the way from the center leading to the mountain Pirin is the street where you can find everything you need in Bansko.

  3. The Old Town

    Near the center of Bansko you can find The Old Town, you will feel there relaxed atmosphere and the history of the town.
    There you can find also some of the best Taverns in Bansko and taste some of the specials of the town.
    You can learn more about the taverns here.

  4. House-Museum Neofit Rilski

    Neofit Rilski is the founder of the Bulgarian secular education as well the first Bulgarian encyclopedist and an important
    figure in the Bulgarian Renaissance. The houser is turned into a museum at 18th century.

  5. House-Museum Nikola Vaptsarov

    The house-museum Nikola Vaptsarov is the house he grew. From the visit you can follow the life of the poet, you can see
    some of his original poems. You can also watch short videos about his life.
